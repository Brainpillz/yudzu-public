This is the public sharing area for selected Obsidian pages.

Only notes copied into 60_Public are published to the website. The rest of the Yudzu vault stays private.

How to publish a page

  1. Duplicate the Obsidian note you want to share.
  2. Move the public copy into 60_Public.
  3. Remove private details, internal names, budgets, passwords, client-sensitive info, and Zenji logs.
  4. Ask Zenji: Publish Obsidian site.
